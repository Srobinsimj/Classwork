📱 History of the Apple iPhone

The Apple iPhone was first unveiled by Steve Jobs on January 9, 2007 at Macworld in San Francisco. It combined a phone, an iPod, and an internet communicator into one device, revolutionizing mobile technology. The first iPhone was released later that year and sold hundreds of thousands of units in its opening weekend. 📌 Key Milestones

2007: First iPhone released.

2008: iPhone 3G introduced with App Store.

2010: iPhone 4 launched with Retina Display.

2014: iPhone 6 and 6 Plus brought larger screens.

2017: iPhone X introduced Face ID and OLED display.

2025: Apple released the iPhone 17 lineup.
